  • Take the New Jersey Turnpike South to Exit 4 (NJ-73).

  • Cross the Betsy Ross Bridge and follow signs for I-95 South.
  • Follow I-95 South to I-676 West.
  • Follow I-676 West to the I-76 East exit (Philadelphia Int'l Airport).
  • Follow I-76 East to Exit 345 (University City/30th Street Station). Merge right and continue up the ramp.
