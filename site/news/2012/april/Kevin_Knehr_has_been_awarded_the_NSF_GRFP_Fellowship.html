April 2nd, 2012 - Kevin Knehr Has Been Awarded the NSF GRFP Fellowship

ECSL member, Kevin Knehr has been awarded the NSF GRFP (Graduate Research Fellowship Program) Fellowship. This is a very competitive and prestigious fellowship, awarded to the top students in the United States to pursue an advanced degree.

Kevin was also awarded Goldwater Scholarship last year for his outstanding achievements as an undergraduate student.

Congratulations Kevin !
